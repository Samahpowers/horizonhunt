Our Main Objective


As an employment consultancy, we provide important work to job seekers and provide the workforce that local
and International employers need to succeed. We pride ourselves on
offering the best service to our clients and helping our job candidates find the work they require. We began our business by servicing Mombasa county and Nairobi area but quickly expanded to include more locations across all the 47 counties. We are located in Upperhill 3rd avenue Opposite 316 Chambers and sub-branches in all other counties. We routinely provide staffing services for clients Whether you are looking for work, or looking to hire, we are here to help. Our experienced staffing

coordinators are ready to help and answer your questions.

Our Global HR Management & Consultancy Options
